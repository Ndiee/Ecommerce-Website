About Us


Welcome to Shoe Haven!

Shoe Haven is your go-to destination for fashionable, high-quality footwear.

Established in 2024, we are committed to providing our customers with shoes that combine comfort, style, and durability.


Our Story

Our journey began in early 2024 with a simple mission: to redefine the shoe shopping experience.

Founders Anderson Chris P. Balaguer and Andrhea Jermane Montero, both passionate about fashion and footwear, sought to create a

store that offers not only the latest trends but also timeless classics. With their extensive backgrounds

in retail and design, they have built a brand that prides itself on excellence and customer satisfaction.


Our Vision

We aim to be a leading name in the footwear industry by offering a curated collection of shoes that cater

to every style and occasion. Our vision is to make Stride & Style synonymous with quality, innovation,

and customer-centricity.
